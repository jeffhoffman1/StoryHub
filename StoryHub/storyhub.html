Proposal

The Pitch

- Great products that are made in the modern world pass through a fine oiled machine of specialists, supply chains and are constantly modified by the demands of a free market. However there are some products that don’t pass through this refining process that could greatly enhance them – for instance books. Books are often static products that usually are crafted by one person or a small group of people who have authoritarian control over how the story is developed. If books were instead an open source work that was open to revision and reproof reading would no longer be a a passive activity but fans and enthusiasts would be able to let their ideas also flow into the book and through interacting with other readers through comments, criticisms and suggestions they will have a change to embellish, extend and enhance the plot and contribute to it's unfolding. People have a natural desire to critique and improve, they are curious what other people think, they want to fill in the blanks and they like the ability to suggest approval or disapproval. This app would take those human attributes and use them to improve the story writing process and make reading a more interactive experience.

What needs will this meet

- Our app uses a wiki based approach to story development which will allow users to edit, add and remove text. It will also act as an interface between users reading the same text so that they can communicate and create a flow of ideas and suggestions that will enable them to critique and collaboratively revise the text which will not only enhance the reading experience but also will be a great tool in creating rich literature. It's like github for stories.
